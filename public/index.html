Humany Skeleton Callback plugin

  1. Open the Widget by clicking the icon in the lower right
  2. Click "Contact us"
  3. Click "Book a callback"
  4. Enter a phone number and select a date
  5. Click "Send"
We have included a mock service to handle submissions and opening hours. To trigger a form error, enter a phone number shorter than 4 digits.
You can also change opening hours parameters in ./src/settings.js Happy coding!
